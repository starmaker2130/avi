Our current internet paradigm is fundamentally based on compromise: we access free software and services in exchange for being sold as a product to the highest bidding advertiser. Is that really the best we can do?


The House of Venus public Augmented Reality kinectome, or pARk, is a standard for an immersive internet managed by its users. It combines mesh networking, IoT, and augmented reality to preserve personal freedoms online and unleash the true potential of widespread digital communication.


In the pARk, your hand is the mouse and the world is your desktop. Time for us to eVolve.

connect via the creative Augmented Reality desktop (cARd), a secure home for all your immersive networking needs and creative projects

Augmented Reality kinectors (ARks) form the backbone of the pARk by hosting and distributing online content in both flat and immersive formats

ARks can be embedded in other devices, like eV, to expand coverage, bringing the immersive web potentially everywhere
